Why We Love The Big Mountains

Mountians are majestic in thier size, beauty, and scope. This site is created to share our love and passion for being in the moutains, on the mountains, and with the mountains. Ultra Endurance Mountain Hiking is roughly defined as any route through the mountians that is undertaken in at least some semi-automony, apporximately averages 350ft vertical per mile, roughly 100 miles or greater, which is pursed with minimal sleep in the sense of a single push. Ultra Endurance Mountain Hiking leverages skills learned from the disciplines of running, trail running, fast packing, ultra running, multi-day running, journey running, hiking, and mountaineering. No suffering in the moutains is required, but it could very well happen. This page and the Order of the Aoudad page are designed to prepare the aspiring Ultra Endurance Mountain Hiker with varying levels of difficulty allowing for self-paced skill building. We encourage you to push yourself in the mountains, but also keep top of mind that the mountians are unforgiving. Assess your skills fairly and honestly, and please don't over reach to early.
